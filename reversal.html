This tool is a working draft companion to the proposed Trust algorithm and does not replace clinical judgement, the SmPC, or the Consultant Haematologist on-call. PCC use for factor Xa inhibitor reversal is off-label in the UK. Andexanet alfa is licensed for life-threatening or uncontrolled bleeding on apixaban or rivaroxaban (NICE TA697; GI bleed only since the January 2025 termination of TA1029 for ICH). Each application is an individual clinical decision by the responsible Consultant.
